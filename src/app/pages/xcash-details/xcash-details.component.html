What it is

XCash Labs is revitalizing X-CASH, originally launched in 2018 as a fork of Monero, with a renewed focus on stability, transparent governance, and long-term sustainability. The native asset of the XCash Labs network is XLB, launched with no premine and no airdrops, preserving the original principles of open, community-driven cryptocurrency.

Core features

  • Flexible privacy with support for both transparent and private transactions
  • DPOPS staking with VRF-based producer selection for fair, community-governed security
  • Open governance and community-led development
  • Future support for cross-chain asset wrapping and minting

Vision

The long-term vision of XCash Labs is to provide a stable, privacy-capable blockchain governed by its community and built to endure. Rather than chasing rapid feature expansion, the project prioritizes correctness, transparency, and steady evolution-ensuring the network
