Welcome to the X-CASH Community

X-CASH is a community-driven blockchain offering flexible privacy, secure delegation, and strong consensus—built for a transparent yet adaptable on-chain ecosystem designed for long-term resilience.

Launched in 2018 as a fork of Monero, X-CASH introduced flex-privacy and later evolved with DPOPS, a VRF-based delegated consensus model that enables fair, community-governed network security.

Following this release, development slowed and the project entered a period of inactivity as the original maintainers stepped away. Today, X-CASH is being revitalized by the community with a renewed focus on stability, transparency, and long-term sustainability—built on proven technology and guided by decentralized governance.

Current development is focused on modernizing and simplifying the protocol, strengthening DPOPS and VRF-based consensus, and expanding interoperability through token wrapping and minting on other chains—laying the foundation for a stable, community-governed ecosystem designed for long-term growth.
